About the Foundation

Built on Purpose,
Driven by Heart

The Letsile Tebogo Charitable Foundation was established by Botswana's first Olympic gold medalist with a singular vision: to give back to the community that helped shape a champion.

We believe sport is more than competition — it's a platform for change. Through mentorship, cancer awareness, and community upliftment, we are committed to building a stronger, healthier Botswana.

"Committed to lifting our community one project at a time."

What We Stand For

Three Pillars of Impact

🤝

Community Upliftment

We invest directly in communities across Botswana — providing essential resources, healthcare supplies, and support to those who need it most. From colostomy bags to school programmes, we meet communities where they are.

🎗️

Cancer Advocacy

We raise awareness around cancer, reducing stigma and ensuring patients receive the care and dignity they deserve. No one should face illness without support.

🏃

Athlete Mentorship

Using the power of sport as a tool for youth development, we mentor the next generation of Botswana's athletes — building character, discipline, and ambition both on and off the track.

Colostomy Bag Distribution Drive

Across Botswana, individuals living with ostomies face critical shortages of essential medical supplies. The LTCF is sourcing and distributing Coloplast SenSura Flex & Click ostomy bags to patients in need — including couriering supplies to those in remote areas who cannot travel.

This initiative is part of our broader cancer advocacy mission: ensuring that patients recovering from colorectal cancer or related conditions can live with dignity and independence.

Scope Nationwide Botswana
Delivery In-person & Courier
Status ● Ongoing

Need Ostomy Supplies?

If you or someone you know requires colostomy bags and cannot access them, reach out to us directly. We will arrange delivery across Botswana where necessary.
